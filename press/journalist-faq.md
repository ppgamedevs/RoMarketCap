## General Questions

### What is RoMarketCap?

RoMarketCap is the reference platform for data on private companies in Romania. We provide daily updated ROMC scores, forecasts, and complete data for over [X] private companies.

### What is a ROMC score?

ROMC (Romanian Market Cap) score is a standardized score from 0 to 100 that evaluates private companies based on financial data, growth signals, and market indicators. Scores are updated daily and provide a way to compare companies across industries and regions.

### How is ROMC score calculated?

ROMC scores are calculated using a proprietary algorithm that considers:
- Financial data (revenue, profit, employees)
- Growth signals (hiring, web traffic, press mentions)
- Market indicators (industry trends, regional factors)
- Data confidence levels

Full methodology is available at: romarketcap.ro/methodology

### How accurate is the data?

We provide confidence levels for all data points:
- **High confidence (80-100%):** Official sources (ANAF, public records)
- **Medium confidence (50-79%):** Enriched data (web scraping, APIs)
- **Low confidence (<50%):** Estimated or incomplete data

All data is clearly marked with confidence levels.

## Methodology Questions

### How do you ensure data accuracy?

We use multiple validation methods:
- Cross-reference multiple sources
- Validate against official records
- Provide confidence levels
- Allow companies to claim and correct data
- Regular data quality audits

### What if a company disputes their data?

Companies can claim their profile and submit corrections. We review all claims and update data accordingly.

### How do forecasts work?

Forecasts use historical ROMC score trends, industry benchmarks, and market indicators to project 90/180 day ROMC score evolution. Forecasts are clearly marked as projections, not guarantees.
