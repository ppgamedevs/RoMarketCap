## Personalization Guidelines

### Research First
- Read their recent articles
- Understand their beat/topic
- Find relevant angle

### Customize
- Mention specific article/topic
- Explain relevance to their work
- Provide specific data examples

### Keep It Short
- Max 200 words
- Clear value proposition
- Easy to scan

### Follow Up
- If no response in 7 days, send one follow-up
- If still no response, move on
- Don't spam

## When NOT to Send

- ❌ If they don't write about relevant topics
