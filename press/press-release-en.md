## RoMarketCap Launches Reference Platform for Market Visibility of Private Companies in Romania

**Bucharest, [DATE]** - RoMarketCap, the first comprehensive data platform for private companies in Romania, announces its official launch. The platform provides daily updated ROMC scores, forecasts, and complete data for over [X] private companies in Romania.

### Context

Romania has over 1.2 million private companies, but until now there was no platform providing systematic market visibility. Founders don't know how they compare to competitors, investors can't find dealflow efficiently, and analysts and journalists lack complete data for reports and articles.

### RoMarketCap Solution

RoMarketCap solves this problem by providing:

- **Daily updated ROMC scores** - Standardized score (0-100) for evaluating private companies
- **90/180 day forecasts** - Projections for ROMC score evolution
- **Complete database** - Over [X] private companies with updated data
- **Investor tools** - Watchlists, alerts, CSV/JSON exports
- **API for integration** - Programmatic data access for B2B

### Impact

RoMarketCap helps:

- **Founders** understand their market position and improve ROMC scores
- **Investors** find automated dealflow and perform rapid due diligence
- **Analysts and journalists** have complete, citable data for reports and articles
- **The ecosystem** become more transparent and efficient
