## Qualification Checklist

Before sending, verify:
- ✅ Journalist writes about relevant topics
- ✅ Journalist has written about Romanian companies/startups
- ✅ Publication is reputable
- ✅ Contact is active journalist
- ✅ No recent negative coverage
- ✅ Not competitor publication

## Follow-Up Rules

- **First follow-up:** 7 days if no response
- **Second follow-up:** 14 days if still no response
- **Maximum:** 2 follow-ups total
- **If no response:** Remove from active list, mark as "no interest"
