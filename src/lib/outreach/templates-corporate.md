## Qualification Checklist

Before sending, verify:
- ✅ Company operates in Romania or invests in Romania
- ✅ Company has BD/M&A/innovation team
- ✅ Company is actively looking for partnerships/acquisitions
- ✅ Contact person is relevant (BD manager, M&A analyst, innovation lead)
- ✅ No recent negative interactions
- ✅ Company is not competitor

- **First follow-up:** 7 days if no response
- **Second follow-up:** 14 days if still no response
- **Maximum:** 2 follow-ups total
- **If no response:** Remove from active list, mark as "no interest"
