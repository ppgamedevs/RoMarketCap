# Target Qualification Checklist

**Purpose:** Ensure every outreach email earns its existence. No spam.

---

## Universal Qualification Criteria

### Must Have (All Must Be True)
- ✅ **Relevant to RoMarketCap:** Target uses or needs company data
- ✅ **Active:** Target is currently active (not defunct, not dormant)
- ✅ **Reachable:** Valid contact information available
- ✅ **No recent negative interaction:** No recent spam reports or negative feedback
- ✅ **Not competitor:** Target is not a direct competitor

### Should Have (At Least 2 Must Be True)
- ✅ **Relevant industry:** Target operates in industries we cover
- ✅ **Relevant geography:** Target operates in or invests in Romania
- ✅ **Relevant size:** Target has sufficient scale to benefit from our data
- ✅ **Relevant use case:** Target has clear use case for our data
- ✅ **Relevant role:** Contact person has decision-making authority

---

## ICP-Specific Qualification

### Funds
**Must Have:**
- ✅ Invests in Romanian companies
- ✅ Invests in relevant industry/stage
- ✅ Has active investment activity (last 12 months)
- ✅ Contact is partner, principal, or analyst

**Should Have:**
- ✅ Fund size >€1M
- ✅ Portfolio includes Romanian companies
- ✅ Actively sourcing deals
- ✅ Uses data tools for due diligence

### Accelerators
**Must Have:**
- ✅ Operates in Romania
- ✅ Has active program (current or upcoming cohort)
- ✅ Focuses on relevant industry/stage
- ✅ Contact is program manager or partner

**Should Have:**
- ✅ Portfolio size >10 companies
- ✅ Active mentorship program
- ✅ Regular demo days or events
- ✅ Helps companies with fundraising

### Media
**Must Have:**
- ✅ Writes about Romanian companies/startups
- ✅ Publication is reputable
- ✅ Contact is active journalist
- ✅ Writes about relevant topics

**Should Have:**
- ✅ Has written about Romanian tech/startups recently
- ✅ Publication has significant readership
- ✅ Uses data in articles
- ✅ Cites sources in articles

### Corporate Innovation
**Must Have:**
- ✅ Operates in Romania or invests in Romania
- ✅ Has BD/M&A/innovation team
- ✅ Actively looking for partnerships/acquisitions
- ✅ Contact is BD manager, M&A analyst, or innovation lead

**Should Have:**
- ✅ Company size >100 employees
- ✅ Annual revenue >€10M
- ✅ Has made acquisitions/partnerships recently
- ✅ Uses data tools for scouting

## Disqualification Criteria

### Immediate Disqualification
- ❌ **Spam complaints:** Target has complained about spam
- ❌ **Opt-out:** Target has explicitly opted out
- ❌ **Competitor:** Target is direct competitor
- ❌ **Irrelevant:** Target has no use case for our data
- ❌ **Inactive:** Target is defunct or dormant
- ❌ **Invalid contact:** No valid contact information

### Temporary Disqualification
- ❌ **Recent outreach:** Contacted within last 90 days
- ❌ **Negative response:** Received negative response
- ❌ **No response:** No response after 2 follow-ups
- ❌ **Wrong contact:** Contact person is not relevant

## Qualification Process

### Step 1: Research
1. Research target company/organization
2. Verify relevance to RoMarketCap
3. Check for recent activity
4. Identify decision-maker contact

### Step 2: Verify
1. Verify contact information
2. Check for opt-out status
3. Check for recent interactions
4. Verify qualification criteria

### Step 3: Qualify
1. Score qualification criteria (must have + should have)
2. Calculate qualification score
3. Decision: Send / Don't send / Research more

### Step 4: Send
1. Personalize email template
2. Send email
3. Track in CRM
4. Set follow-up reminder

---

## Qualification Score

### Scoring System
- **Must Have:** All must be true (pass/fail)
- **Should Have:** Score 1 point each (max 5 points)
- **Minimum Score:** 2 points to qualify

### Decision Matrix
- **Score 0-1:** Don't send (not qualified)
- **Score 2-3:** Send (qualified)
- **Score 4-5:** Priority send (highly qualified)

## CRM Tracking

### Required Fields
- **Target Name:** Company/organization name
- **Contact Name:** Contact person name
- **Contact Email:** Contact email address
- **ICP Type:** Funds / Accelerators / Media / Corporate
- **Qualification Score:** 0-5 score
- **Status:** Qualified / Not Qualified / Pending
- **Last Contact:** Date of last outreach
- **Response Status:** No Response / Interested / Not Interested
- **Notes:** Additional notes

### Optional Fields
- **Company Size:** Number of employees
- **Industry:** Primary industry
- **Location:** City, county
- **Website:** Company website
- **LinkedIn:** Company LinkedIn URL
- **Relevant Info:** Any relevant information

## Follow-Up Rules

### Timing
- **First follow-up:** 7 days after initial email
- **Second follow-up:** 14 days after first follow-up
- **Maximum:** 2 follow-ups total

### Conditions
- **Send follow-up if:** No response and still qualified
- **Don't send follow-up if:** Negative response, opt-out, or disqualified

### Follow-Up Content
- **First follow-up:** Brief reminder, offer to help
- **Second follow-up:** Final attempt, offer alternative (newsletter, etc.)

### After Follow-Ups
- **If no response:** Mark as "no interest", remove from active list
- **If negative response:** Mark as "not interested", respect opt-out
- **If positive response:** Move to next stage (call, demo, etc.)

## Anti-Spam Guidelines

### Email Rules
- **Personalize:** Always personalize emails
- **Relevant:** Only send to qualified targets
- **Value:** Always provide value in email
- **Opt-out:** Always include unsubscribe option

### Frequency Limits
- **Per target:** Max 1 email per 90 days
- **Per ICP:** Max 10 emails per day per ICP
- **Total:** Max 30 emails per day total

### Content Rules
- **No spam triggers:** Avoid spam words
- **Clear subject:** Descriptive, not clickbait
- **Relevant content:** Content must be relevant to target
- **Professional tone:** Professional, not salesy

## Quality Assurance

### Before Sending Checklist
- ✅ Target is qualified (passes all criteria)
- ✅ Email is personalized
- ✅ Contact information is correct
- ✅ No recent negative interactions
- ✅ Email provides value
- ✅ Follow-up plan is set

### After Sending Checklist
- ✅ Email tracked in CRM
- ✅ Follow-up reminder set
- ✅ Response tracked
- ✅ Status updated

## Success Metrics

### Outreach Metrics
- **Qualification Rate:** % of researched targets that qualify
- **Response Rate:** % of emails that receive response
- **Positive Response Rate:** % of responses that are positive
- **Conversion Rate:** % of positive responses that convert

### Quality Metrics
- **Spam Complaint Rate:** % of emails that receive spam complaints
- **Opt-Out Rate:** % of emails that result in opt-outs
- **Bounce Rate:** % of emails that bounce
- **Engagement Rate:** % of emails that receive opens/clicks
