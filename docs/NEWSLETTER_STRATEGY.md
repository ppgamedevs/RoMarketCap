## Email Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- **Keep it short:** 40-50 characters
- **Be specific:** Include week dates or topic
- **Create urgency:** "This week's movers" not "Market update"
- **Avoid spam words:** "Free", "Guaranteed", "Act now"

### Content
- **Scannable:** Use headers, bullet points, short paragraphs
- **Visual:** Include charts/graphs where possible
- **Actionable:** Clear CTAs with benefits
- **Personalized:** Use subscriber name, preferences

### Timing
- **Weekly digest:** Monday morning (9 AM Romania time)
- **Welcome sequence:** Immediate, Day 2, Day 5
- **Avoid:** Weekends, holidays, late evening

### Mobile Optimization
- **Single column layout**
- **Large buttons** (min 44x44px)
- **Short subject lines**
- **Preheader text** (first line of email)

## Newsletter Growth Tactics

### Acquisition
1. **CTAs on all pages** - Newsletter signup forms
2. **Incentives** - "Exclusive rankings" for subscribers
3. **Social proof** - Subscriber count, testimonials
4. **Exit intent** - Popup on exit (if not annoying)

### Retention
1. **Consistent delivery** - Weekly, same day/time
2. **Valuable content** - Always provide insights
3. **Personalization** - Industry/county preferences
4. **Re-engagement** - Win-back emails for inactive subscribers

### Conversion
1. **Premium CTAs** - In every email
2. **Exclusive content** - Premium-only insights
3. **Social proof** - Premium subscriber testimonials
4. **Urgency** - Limited-time offers (if ethical)

### Referral
1. **Referral links** - In every email
2. **Incentive messaging** - Clear benefits
3. **Easy sharing** - One-click share buttons
4. **Tracking** - Monitor referral conversions

## Email Automation

### Welcome Sequence
- **Trigger:** Newsletter subscription confirmed
- **Emails:** 3 emails (Day 0, Day 2, Day 5)
- **Goal:** Onboard and convert

### Re-engagement Sequence
- **Trigger:** No opens for 30 days
- **Emails:** 2 emails (Day 30, Day 45)
- **Goal:** Re-engage or unsubscribe

### Win-back Sequence
- **Trigger:** Unsubscribed but premium user
- **Emails:** 1 email (Day 7)
- **Goal:** Win back subscriber
