# Growth Metrics Dashboard

**Last Updated:** 2024  
**Status:** Strategy Locked

---

## Overview

This document defines RoMarketCap's growth metrics framework. We focus on truth, not vanity metrics. Every metric tells us what to double down on or what to kill without emotion.

---

## North-Star Metrics

### Primary North-Star Metric

**Weekly Active Users (WAU) with Premium Intent**

**Definition:** Users who visit RoMarketCap at least once per week AND show premium intent (view pricing, click premium CTA, or use premium-gated features).

**Why This Matters:**
- Measures true engagement, not just traffic
- Correlates directly with revenue potential
- Shows product-market fit
- Indicates sustainable growth

**Target:** [To be set based on baseline]

**Measurement:**
- Track weekly unique users
- Filter for premium intent actions
- Calculate WAU with premium intent
- Compare week-over-week

---

### Secondary North-Star Metrics

**1. Premium Conversion Rate**

**Definition:** % of users who sign up for Premium within 30 days of first visit.

**Target:** 5-10% (varies by ICP)

**Why This Matters:**
- Direct revenue indicator
- Shows product value
- Measures monetization efficiency

**2. Newsletter-to-Premium Conversion Rate**

**Definition:** % of newsletter subscribers who convert to Premium within 90 days.

**Target:** 2-5%

**Why This Matters:**
- Measures newsletter value
- Shows engagement quality
- Indicates retention potential

**3. Referral Rate**

**Definition:** % of users who refer others within 30 days of signup.

**Target:** 10-15%

**Why This Matters:**
- Measures product satisfaction
- Shows organic growth potential
- Indicates product-market fit

## Metric Definitions

### Engagement Metrics

**Weekly Active Users (WAU):**
- Users who visit at least once per week
- Measured: Weekly unique users

**Premium Intent:**
- Actions that indicate premium interest:
  - View pricing page
  - Click premium CTA
  - Use premium-gated feature
  - View premium features

**First Action:**
- First meaningful action after signup:
  - Search for company
  - View company page
  - Claim company
  - Add to watchlist

---

### Revenue Metrics

**Premium Conversion Rate:**
- % of users who sign up for Premium within 30 days

**Premium Retention Rate:**
- % of Premium users who renew subscription

**Average Revenue Per User (ARPU):**
- Total revenue / Number of Premium users

**Lifetime Value (LTV):**
- Average revenue per user over lifetime
- Calculated: ARPU × Average subscription duration

---

### Retention Metrics

**Week N Retention:**
- % of users who return in week N after signup

**Premium Retention:**
- % of Premium users who renew subscription

**Churn Rate:**
- % of users who cancel subscription
- Calculated: Cancellations / Total Premium users
