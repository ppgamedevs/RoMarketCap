### 2. Top 100 Private Companies

**Purpose:** Create a definitive ranking that journalists and analysts reference

**Methodology:**

#### Selection Criteria
1. **ROMC Score:** Primary ranking factor (0-100)
2. **Data Confidence:** Minimum 70% confidence required
3. **Data Completeness:** Must have revenue, employees, or both
4. **Public Status:** Must be public companies (not hidden)
5. **Active Status:** Must be active companies

#### Ranking Factors
1. **ROMC Score (70% weight):** Current ROMC score
2. **Growth Trend (20% weight):** ROMC score change over 90 days
3. **Data Confidence (10% weight):** Confidence level in data

#### Data Requirements
- **Company Name:** Legal name and trade name
- **CUI:** Romanian unique identifier
- **Industry:** CAEN code and description
- **County:** Location
- **ROMC Score:** Current score (0-100)
- **Revenue:** Latest revenue figure (if available)
- **Employees:** Employee count (if available)
- **Founded:** Year of establishment

#### Update Frequency
- **Weekly:** ROMC scores updated
- **Monthly:** Full ranking refreshed
- **Quarterly:** Methodology reviewed

#### Publication Format
- **Website:** Interactive ranking table
- **PDF:** Downloadable report
- **API:** Programmatic access
- **Embed:** Embeddable widget

#### Media Kit
- **Press Release:** Monthly announcement
- **Data Package:** CSV/JSON download
- **Visualizations:** Charts and graphs
- **Quotes:** Ready-to-use quotes from team

**Publication:** Monthly ranking, updated weekly  
**Distribution:** Website, newsletter, press releases, media outreach  
**Media Kit:** PDF, CSV, JSON, embeddable charts

### 3. Weekly Movers Commentary

**Purpose:** Provide authoritative commentary on market movements that journalists reference

**Commentary Format:**

#### Structure
1. **Executive Summary** (2-3 sentences)
   - Key findings
   - Notable movements
   - Market context

2. **Top Increases Analysis** (3-4 paragraphs)
   - Which companies moved up
   - Why they moved up (if known)
   - Industry/county context
   - What this indicates

3. **Top Decreases Analysis** (3-4 paragraphs)
   - Which companies moved down
   - Why they moved down (if known)
   - Industry/county context
   - What this indicates

4. **Industry Trends** (2-3 paragraphs)
   - Which industries moved most
   - Industry-specific insights
   - Comparative analysis

5. **County Trends** (2-3 paragraphs)
   - Which counties moved most
   - Regional insights
   - Comparative analysis

6. **Market Commentary** (2-3 paragraphs)
   - Overall market trends
   - What these movements indicate
   - Context and insights
   - Forward-looking statements

#### Commentary Guidelines
- **Data-driven:** All commentary must be backed by data
- **Neutral tone:** No opinions, just facts and analysis
- **Transparent:** Cite sources, admit limitations
- **Educational:** Help readers understand market movements
- **Actionable:** Provide insights readers can use

#### Forbidden Phrases
- ❌ "Investors should" (financial advice)
- ❌ "Market crash" or "market boom" (too dramatic)
- ❌ "Guaranteed growth" (no guarantees)
- ❌ Speculative claims without data
- ❌ Investment recommendations

#### Authoritative Language
- ✅ "Data shows..."
- ✅ "According to ROMC scores..."
- ✅ "Analysis indicates..."
- ✅ "Trends suggest..."
- ✅ "Based on [X] companies..."

**Publication:** Weekly commentary, published Monday  
**Distribution:** Website, newsletter, social media  
**Media Kit:** Ready-to-use quotes, embeddable commentary
