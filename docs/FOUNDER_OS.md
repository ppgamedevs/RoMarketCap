## "What Not to Do" List

### Product

**❌ Don't Build Features Users Don't Want**
- Always validate before building
- Use data, not opinions
- Kill features that don't work

**❌ Don't Optimize for Vanity Metrics**
- Focus on revenue metrics
- Ignore traffic without conversion
- Measure what matters

**❌ Don't Copy Competitors Blindly**
- Understand why features exist
- Test before copying
- Focus on differentiation

**❌ Don't Ignore User Feedback**
- Listen to users
- But validate with data
- Don't build everything users ask for

---

### Growth

**❌ Don't Spend on Channels That Don't Convert**
- Track ROI for all channels
- Kill channels that don't work
- Double down on winners

**❌ Don't A/B Test Without Hypotheses**
- Every test needs a hypothesis
- Measure what matters
- Don't test randomly

**❌ Don't Ignore Retention**
- Acquisition is expensive
- Retention is free
- Focus on retention first

**❌ Don't Scale Before Product-Market Fit**
- Find PMF first
- Then scale
- Don't scale prematurely

---

### Operations

**❌ Don't Hire Too Fast**
- Hire for need, not growth
- Quality over quantity
- Don't hire until you need to

**❌ Don't Ignore Unit Economics**
- Track unit economics always
- Don't grow unprofitably
- Focus on sustainable growth

**❌ Don't Ignore Cash Flow**
- Monitor cash flow weekly
- Don't run out of cash
- Plan for runway

**❌ Don't Ignore Legal/Compliance**
- Stay compliant
- Don't ignore regulations
- Get legal advice when needed

---

### Strategy

**❌ Don't Pivot Without Data**
- Use data to make decisions
- Don't pivot on hunches
- Validate before pivoting

**❌ Don't Ignore Competition**
- Monitor competition
- But don't copy blindly
- Focus on differentiation

**❌ Don't Ignore Market Trends**
- Stay aware of trends
- But don't chase every trend
- Focus on your mission

**❌ Don't Ignore Team Morale**
- Monitor team morale
- Don't ignore burnout
- Take care of your team

## Decision Framework

### When to Make Decisions

**Immediate Decisions:**
- Security issues
- Legal issues
- Critical bugs
- Data loss

**Quick Decisions (1-2 days):**
- Product changes
- Marketing changes
- Hiring decisions
- Partnership decisions

**Strategic Decisions (1-2 weeks):**
- Major pivots
- Major investments
- Exit decisions
- Major hires

---

### How to Make Decisions

**Step 1: Gather Data**
- Collect relevant data
- Analyze metrics
- Review feedback
- Check trends

**Step 2: Analyze Options**
- List all options
- Analyze pros/cons
- Check feasibility
- Estimate impact

**Step 3: Make Decision**
- Use data to decide
- Consider team input
- Consider long-term impact
- Document decision

**Step 4: Execute & Monitor**
- Execute decision
- Monitor results
- Adjust if needed
- Document learnings

## Personal Sustainability

### Work-Life Balance

**Work Hours:**
- Target: 50-60 hours/week
- Don't work 24/7
- Take breaks
- Rest on weekends

**Health:**
- Exercise regularly
- Eat well
- Sleep enough
- Manage stress

**Relationships:**
- Maintain relationships
- Don't ignore family/friends
- Take time off
- Enjoy life

---

### Burnout Prevention

**Signs of Burnout:**
- Constant exhaustion
- Loss of motivation
- Decreased performance
- Negativity

**Prevention:**
- Take breaks
- Delegate tasks
- Set boundaries
- Ask for help

**If Burned Out:**
- Take time off
- Reassess priorities
- Get support
- Don't ignore it
