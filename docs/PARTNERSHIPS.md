# Partnerships & Integrations Strategy

**Last Updated:** 2024  
**Status:** Strategy Locked

---

## Overview

RoMarketCap's partnership strategy focuses on letting others sell RoMarketCap for us. We partner with organizations that can distribute our data, refer users, or integrate our API. This document defines partner categories, revenue share models, and API positioning.

---

## Partner Categories

### 1. Accounting Firms

**Value Proposition:** Provide clients with market intelligence and benchmarking

**Partnership Model:**
- **Data Access:** Discounted Premium subscriptions for accounting firm clients
- **White-Label:** Option to white-label RoMarketCap data (if applicable)
- **Referral Program:** Revenue share for referrals
- **Integration:** API access for CRM integration

**Use Cases:**
- Benchmark client companies against market
- Provide market intelligence reports to clients
- Identify growth opportunities for clients
- Track client company performance

**Revenue Share:**
- **Referral Fee:** 20% of first-year subscription revenue
- **Volume Discount:** 30% discount for 10+ subscriptions
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for enterprise deals

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 50 clients
- Active accounting practice in Romania
- Interest in market intelligence

## Revenue Share Models

### Model 1: Referral Program

**Structure:**
- Partner refers users to RoMarketCap
- User signs up for Premium via partner link
- Partner receives commission on first-year revenue

**Commission Rates:**
- **Standard:** 20% of first-year subscription revenue
- **Volume:** 25% for 10+ referrals per month
- **Enterprise:** 30% for enterprise deals

**Payment Terms:**
- Monthly payments
- Minimum payout: €50
- Paid within 30 days of user payment

---

### Model 2: White-Label (Future)

**Structure:**
- Partner white-labels RoMarketCap data
- Partner sells under their brand
- Revenue share on all sales

**Commission Rates:**
- **Standard:** 50% revenue share
- **Volume:** 60% for high-volume partners
- **Custom:** Negotiated for enterprise

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 100 users
- Brand alignment
- Technical integration capability

---

### Model 3: API Licensing

**Structure:**
- Partner licenses RoMarketCap API
- Partner integrates data into their product
- Revenue share on API usage

**Pricing:**
- **Standard:** €X per API call
- **Volume:** Discounted rates for high volume
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing

**Requirements:**
- Technical integration capability
- Minimum API usage commitment
- Brand alignment

**Use Cases:**

- **Company Data:** Complete company profiles
- **ROMC Scores:** Daily updated scores
- **Forecasts:** 90/180 day projections
- **Search:** Advanced search and filtering
- **Export:** CSV/JSON export capabilities

**Pricing:**
- **Starter:** €X/month for X API calls
- **Professional:** €Y/month for Y API calls
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing

**Requirements:**
- Technical integration capability
- Minimum usage commitment
- Brand alignment
- Data usage agreement
